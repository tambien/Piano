Multisampled Piano

This is a demonstration of a multisampled piano build with Tone.js. You can hear it by either plugging in a midi keyboard, or hit 'PLAY' to hear a midi file.


It uses the Salamander Grand Piano Samples which includes high quality stereo samples of every third key at up to 16 velocity levels, release harmonics sampled in thirds, damper sounds (on the note release), and sustain pedal sounds.
